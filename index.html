BIO

I am a 3D Character Modeler with roughly four years of experience, I'm italian and I'm currently based in France.
My main softwares are Maya and Zbrush, and I have worked in cartoon character development for both Tv series and commercials.
‍
I’m a space geek (space!) and I am very passionate about videogames as well: I was playing before I could read. So much for tutorials.
I am a team player with leadership skills. I am also strong in problem solving and in the fine art of Bodging. 
‍
Also, this picture portraits me moments before extracting Excalibur and becoming Queen of England.

What I Do

I specialize in crafting high-quality 3D assets for various projects, ranging from games to visual effects. My work involves meticulously modeling, texturing, and lighting, ensuring each asset aligns with the project's artistic vision. I also focus on optimizing these assets for performance without compromising on detail. In addition to asset creation, I collaborate closely with other artists and designers, contributing to the development of efficient pipelines and workflows that enhance overall project efficiency and quality

Who I Am

I am a skilled 3D artist with expertise in creating detailed and visually compelling 3D assets. My experience includes collaborating with multidisciplinary teams, optimizing workflows, and ensuring that every asset meets the highest standards of quality and creativity. I am committed to pushing the boundaries of 3D artistry, constantly refining my techniques to deliver outstanding results.
